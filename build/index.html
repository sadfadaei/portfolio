Research Projects

A selection of my recent work

Satellite Networks Measurement Starlink
🏆 Best of CCR · ACM SIGCOMM 2025

LEOScope: Global LEO Testbed

A scalable global testbed measuring network performance across LEO satellite providers — spanning from Northern Canada to Antarctica.

  • Deployed probes from Northern Canada to McMurdo Station, Antarctica
  • Long-term framework studying Geomagnetic Storms (Solar Cycle 25)
  • Characterised TCP congestion control over ISL paths
Paper
Measurement Solar Storms LEO/MEO/GEO
ACM SIGMETRICS 2026 — Accepted

Solar Storms & Orbital Internet Resilience

1.5 years of continuous measurement mapping the impact of extreme solar storms across all orbital regimes.

  • Correlated Kp index & X-ray flux with latency, loss, and throughput KPIs
  • Compared Starlink, OneWeb, and GEO providers during X-class flares
  • First large-scale empirical study of its kind
Paper
CDN LEO Video Streaming
ACM HotNets 2024

CDN Architecture for LEO — VDC

Virtual Delivery Constellations (VDC): novel CDN placement strategies optimised for LEO satellite constellation topology.

  • Optimised PoP placement within Starlink-class constellations
  • Power-aware satellite selection for content delivery
  • Break-even analysis vs ground-based CDN
Paper
Privacy ML Browser Extension
ASONAM 2025

Transition Guardian — Privacy Plugin

On-device AI browser plugin that detects and blocks accidental sharing of sensitive self-disclosure data — zero external data leakage.

  • Runs entirely on-device inside the browser
  • Detects minority status indicators before form submission
  • Targets users during high-risk life transitions
Paper
Video Streaming Optimisation CDN
ACM SIGMETRICS 2024

Shrinking VOD Traffic via Rényi-Entropic OT

Optimal transport-based approach to reduce VOD traffic volume using Rényi entropy regularisation.

  • Applied Rényi-entropic optimal transport to video chunk scheduling
  • Significant reduction in redundant VOD traffic
